The web application AutoKNN was part of the thesis of the Department of Information and Electronic Engineering (I.E.E.) at the International Hellenic University (I.H.U.), called "Web application for automated nearest neighbor classification".

This web application was under the supervision of Assistant Profeccor Mr. Stefanos Ougiaroglou.
